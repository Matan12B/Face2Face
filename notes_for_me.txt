video comm:

Host camera
  → JPEG encode
  → split_frame_to_packets (frame_id=42, 8 packets)
  → AES encrypt each packet
  → sendto(guest_ip, 5000)          [UDP × 8]

Guest receives packets (possibly out of order):
  → AES decrypt
  → FrameReassembler.handle_packet()
  → when all 8 arrive → rebuild_frame()
  → cv2.imdecode → OpenCV frame
  → frameQ.put((frame, timestamp, addr))
  → receive_video_loop → av_sync.add_video()
  → playback_loop → remote_video_queue
  → call_frame timer → VideoPanel.set_frame()
